# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built

Governor: a split-panel conversation simulator that lets deployment managers test how an Engineered Arts robot responds to real-world inputs — and surfaces compliance risks in real-time across eight categories (LEGAL, MEDICAL, FIN, PII, HALLUC, TONE, BIAS, SAFETY) as the AI generates each response.

## AI Value Archetype

Oracle — the AI reveals compliance risk that is invisible to the deployment manager without systematic testing of the robot's generative responses.

## The Bet in One Sentence

A simulation and compliance monitoring platform for Engineered Arts robot deployments, giving enterprise operators a true-parity environment to red-team conversation behaviour and catch compliance violations before — and during — live deployment, generating proprietary HRI safety data that no competitor can replicate.

## Kill Criteria

- Existing customers, when interviewed, don't describe compliance or unpredictable AI behaviour as a meaningful pain — they consider it already solved or not their problem
- Customers are satisfied using a generic voice agent (e.g. ElevenLabs) for pre-deployment testing and don't see embodied parity as meaningful
- Engineered Arts engineering confirms that true behavioural parity between simulation and physical robot is not technically achievable within a viable timeframe
- Fewer than 3 existing customers express willingness to pay after seeing the prototype
